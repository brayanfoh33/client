Our History Timeline...

The history of VACOS is a testament to a legacy of excellence and innovation in education. Since its establishment in 2004, the school has celebrated remarkable milestones.

  • The Genesis: March, 2004, Visions Academy Cluster of Schools came into inception with Mr. Acquah Appiah as the founder.
  • Accreditation: August, 2004, VACOS received accreditation from the Ghana Education Service to operate as a fully-fledged institution.
  • Pioneer Learners Enrolled: The educational journey rolled out with twenty pioneering pupils and three educators. Mrs. Agnes Konadu Agyeman was the first pupil to be enrolled.
  • Pioneers Completed Junior High School:Thirteen out of the twenty pioneers who began from Crèche and completed Junior High School in 2017 were: Maame Essi; daughter of the founding father, Amoatey Simon, Bryan Wealth, Samuel-Martins, Osei Anane Abena, etc.

Why Choose Our School?

Our school is committed to academic excellence, fostering a holistic approach that nurtures students’ intellectual, emotional, and social growth. With a dedicated and experienced faculty, we provide a dynamic curriculum designed to prepare students for success in a rapidly changing world. Our state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, science labs, and extracurricular spaces, ensure a conducive environment for both learning and creativity. We prioritize inclusivity, offering a supportive learning environment that values diversity and caters to the unique needs of every student.

We train the whole body...

As we train the mind of our learners for them to become intellectually sharp, we also give them Physical Education to ensure that their entire bodies are fit and strong to support them in achieving their dreams.

Mission Statement...

The mission of the school is to: Guide pupils to think critically and work independently. Encourage maximum parental support and involvement in child-centered education. Empower teachers to have an interactive approach to teaching. Maintain a culture of Reading and Research. Inculcate an acceptable value system.

Boarding and Hostel Facilities...

Our boarding facilities are well structured to give quality supervision, comfort, security, 24-hour electricity and water supply and an academic programme to keep the performance of our boarders high. Our boarding facility is simply a home away from home.
